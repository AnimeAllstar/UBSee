  • How to Use
    Use the dropdown menus in the sidebar to find a subject graph or a course graph. Settings menu can help improve readability of graph.

    The Nodes represent a specific course, ex: CPSC 110.

    The lines between courses represent the prerequisites between courses. Hover over a course for detailed prerequisite information.

    Nodes can be clicked to indicate that the requirement as satisfied.

    Eligible Satisfied Ineligible

    Right click on a node to explore more options.

  • Be Careful!
    This program may not be accurately representative of the actual course requirements displayed on SSC as the information may become outdated. Always refer to SSC information to confirm important information

    Due to graphs becoming too complex, not all the prerequisites are displayed on screen. We recommend always confirming the information on SSC.
